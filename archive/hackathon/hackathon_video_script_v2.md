## 0:25 – 1:00 | THE RELIEF (LLM + Deterministic Mirror)
*Visual: A frantic user types a 30-second brain dump into a blank text box: "i need to finish the BCI paper by 4 but i'm exhausted and i still have to email the investors." They hit 'Enter'.*
*Visual: The text instantly morphs into a perfectly structured, color-coded timeline. Categories assigned. Deadlines bound.*

**VOICEOVER:**
> "It starts with immediate relief. You dump your chaos; our LLM agent structures it instantly. But LyraOS isn't just an interface—it's a deterministic behavioral mirror. It watches how you work. It knows you underestimate academic writing by 40%. It knows your discipline peaks at 10 AM. It silently calculates your Bayesian archetype, dynamically adjusting your schedule so you never overcommit again."

## 1:00 – 1:50 | THE WOW FACTOR (Closing the Loop with BCI)
*Visual: Split screen. The user is deep in focus on their laptop. On the right side of the screen, subtle, beautiful BCI telemetry (SSVEP/P300 waveforms) glows softly.*

**VOICEOVER:**
> "But what happens when you hit a wall? Traditional software waits for you to fail. LyraOS adapts before you do."

*Visual: The BCI waveform shifts colors. The SSVEP signal detects acute cognitive fatigue.*
*Visual: Without the user touching the keyboard, the LyraOS timeline fluidly animates. The heavy "BCI Paper" task slides down the screen. A new, calm block materializes in its place: `Fatigue Detected: 15-Minute Recovery Protocol suggested.`*

**VOICEOVER:**
> "By closing the loop with non-invasive BCI, Lyra reads your cognitive state in real time. It detects your focus dipping. The LLM agent proactively re-prioritizes your queue. You approve the shift with a single glance. No typing. No context switching. Just seamless, continuous flow."

### Why this works (The Antigravity Breakdown):
1. **Ditches the "Architecture Diagram":** Investors and judges don't care about Postgres tables or Phase numbers. They care about *what the product does for the user*. v1 was a technical lecture; v2 is a product pitch.
2. **Sells "The Magic":** The moment the BCI detects fatigue and the UI automatically shifts the schedule—that is the "Holy Grail" of productivity software. We explicitly highlight that it happens *without typing*.
3. **Establishes the Moat:** We explicitly mention "pre-registered behavioral models" and a "deterministic data pipeline." This tells investors/judges: "We aren't just another ChatGPT wrapper. We have rigorous math underneath this."

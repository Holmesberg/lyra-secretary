# Retention as Research Constraint

## Definition

Retention is the precondition for longitudinal behavioral measurement, not a vanity product metric.

## Why It Matters

No user continuity means no useful longitudinal signal.

## Where It Appears

- product friction decisions
- onboarding
- feedback design
- legal/privacy readiness

## Failure Mode

Inference depth harms the data existence condition it depends on.

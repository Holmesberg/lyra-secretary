# Mirror Do Not Judge

## Definition

LyraOS reflects observed behavior neutrally and avoids praise, blame, productivity scores, worth claims, or identity assertions.

## Why It Matters

Judgment changes user behavior, harms trust, and can turn hypotheses into identity labels.

## Where It Appears

- micro-mirrors
- insights
- archetype display
- calibration nudges

## Failure Mode

The product starts evaluating the user instead of reflecting patterns.

# Self Model

This note tracks what kind of system LyraOS is becoming.

## Current Self-Description

LyraOS is becoming a measurement-validity system embedded in a low-friction product. Its highest-risk drift is not technical failure; it is the quiet conversion of hypotheses, policies, and model outputs into trusted reality.

## What This Note Watches

- Whether [[Measurement Validity]] remains the center instead of any one implementation mechanism.
- Whether [[Exposure Ledger]] is treated as an operational gate or as the whole theory.
- Whether [[Cortex]] stays read-only and profile-bound.
- Whether [[Policy as Hypothesis]] remains visible after policies become routine.
- Whether [[Tension Index]] entries still name both valid sides.
- Whether [[Interpretation Drift Log]] records meaning changes, not just feature changes.

## Pressure Axes

- Truth fidelity vs usability
- Automation vs interpretability
- Observation vs intervention
- Low friction vs identifiability
- Helpfulness vs contamination
- Local coherence vs global drift

## Current Drift Watch

- Exposure policy may become invisible truth.
- Exposure Ledger may become over-centralized as a graph hub because it is operationally touched by many systems.
- Operator tooling may appear research-like because it produces structured summaries.
- Product recovery affordances may be mistaken for measured execution.
- AI synthesis may compress disagreement into apparent consensus.

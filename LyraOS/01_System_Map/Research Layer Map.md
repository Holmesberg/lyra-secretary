# Research Layer Map

The research layer interprets product behavior under identifiability constraints. It does not create product semantics.

## Owns

- derived metrics
- clean-data profiles
- hypothesis classification
- uncertainty propagation
- exposure-aware baseline gating

## Must Preserve

- reproducibility
- falsifiability
- unknown propagation
- source provenance

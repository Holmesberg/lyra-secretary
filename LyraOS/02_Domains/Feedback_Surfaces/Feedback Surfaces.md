# Feedback Surfaces

## What It Is

The set of user-visible mirrors, nudges, predictions, chips, and repair prompts that reflect behavior or suggest action.

## Why It Exists

Feedback creates product value by helping users notice patterns, recover from drift, and make better decisions.

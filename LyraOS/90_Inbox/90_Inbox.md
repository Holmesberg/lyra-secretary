# 90_Inbox

Inbox notes are untrusted candidates. They are not durable knowledge until reviewed and promoted.

## Candidate Areas

- `Agent_Candidates/`
- `Repo_Change_Candidates/`
- `Pattern_Candidates/`
- `Tension_Candidates/`
- `Redaction_Candidates/`
- `Unsorted/`

## Promotion Rule

Promote only after source refs, links, uncertainty labels, and the target note type are clear.

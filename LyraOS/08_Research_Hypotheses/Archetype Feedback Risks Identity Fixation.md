# Archetype Feedback Risks Identity Fixation

## Claim

Showing archetype proximity may help reflection but risks users treating time-local hypotheses as identity.

## Observable Signals

- user language adopts archetype labels
- planning changes after archetype exposure
- self-report shifts after archetype display

## Counter-Signals

- users interpret display as provisional pattern only
- no measurable change after exposure

## Clean-Data Requirements

Archetype evidence must exclude exposed or unknown baseline rows.

## Exposure Risks

The archetype mirror may change future behavior or self-description.

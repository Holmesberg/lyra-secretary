# Counterfactual - OpenClaw Treated As Product Data

## Alternative World

OpenClaw orchestration traces are admitted as Lyra product research data.

## Expected Benefit

Rich operator-session data could reveal cognitive continuity failures.

## Expected Harm

Operator tooling, agent output, and implementation traces contaminate user research boundaries.

## What Current Architecture Assumes

Operator runtime must remain outside product research unless a successor contract admits it.

## What Evidence Could Change The Assumption

A future contract defining operator-session analysis, clean-data profiles, consent, and exposure treatment.
